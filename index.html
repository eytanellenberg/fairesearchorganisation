FAIR Research Organization

Independent research group developing causal attribution frameworks across public health, medicine, actuarial science, and sport.

Overview

FAIR (Filtering evidence, Association quantification, Individual partitioning, Reasoned attribution) provides a unified methodology to quantify how multiple factors contribute to outcomes across domains.
